Why choose us?

To provide ethical and professional consultancy services at affordable & competitive fees, for those seeking Canadian Immigration Services or facing challenges related to immigration.

* We are regulated
We are regulated by the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants’ (CICC) strict Code of Conduct that ensures that the interests of the consumers are protected. Under Canadian Immigration Law, only Canadian lawyers and regulated CICC members in good standing, are legally allowed to provide Immigration related advice and represent Clients.


* Timely service
We ensure timely services and help prepare a convincing application to give you the best chances of success in starting your new life in Canada
